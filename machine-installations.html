Frequently Asked Questions

How do you carry out the machine installation services?

Our machine installation services are carried out through a series of planned steps which include site inspection, machine positioning, assembling, connecting to the utilities, proper calibration, and safety checks, followed by a test run to ensure proper operation.

What are the site requirements before installation?

Yes, please ensure that the site is clean and equipped with the necessary accessories, power supply, ventilation and optimal space for the technicians to conduct the installation process without a glitch.

How fast can the machines be installed ?

The installation times depend on the machine types, size and how complex the connections are. While small machines can be installed within a short time period, heavy machines may require hours to days.

Do you provide operator training after installation?

Yes, our technicians will provide guidelines regarding the operation, start and shutdown processes, routine maintenance tips, safety measures and some basics related to troubleshooting.
